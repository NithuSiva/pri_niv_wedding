Itinerary

Vanakkam

an auspicious welcome

Mappilai Alaippu

welcoming the groom

Aalathi

lighting of the lamp

Sankalpam

commencement of the ceremony

Panchakavya Pooja

purifying the groom

Kannika Thaanam

giving away of the bride

Koorai Sari & Thali

blessing the wedding sari and necklace

Thali Kattu

tying the sacred knot

Saptapadi

the seven steps

Asservatham & Vidai

final blessings & bride's farewell
